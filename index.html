Exodus is a Metaverse Role-Playing Game (MRPG) set in the medieval era. The founder of the project played many MMORPG genre games growing up, and along the way made a few lifelong friends. In (insert month & year that the idea for a rpg was first pitched) they decided to work together to create Exodus: Goodbye World, within the Decentraland metaverse that they all began exploring in late 2021.

Out of a shared passion for cryptocurrencies and MMORPGs, Exodus was born with the goal of making these communities and economies into real, consensus driven assets. The name Exodus symbolizes a journey away from the online games we played and cherished as children towards a new era where crypto assets allow gamers to create a real value out of their playtime. We believe that time spent gaming is valuable, and it’s time to start treating it as such. It’s time to Exodus.
