PLAY & CREATE

Exodus is a medieval era metaverse role-playing game built by childhood friends who met online playing a Massively Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Game, or MMORPG. MMORPGs bring together millions of people who form strong bonds, communities, and entire economies. Out of a shared passion for cryptocurrencies and MMORPGs, Exodus was born with the goal of making these communities and economies into real, consensus driven assets. The name Exodus symbolizes a journey away from the online games we played and cherished as children towards a new era where crypto assets allow gamers to create a real value out of their playtime. We believe that time spent gaming is valuable, and it’s time to start treating it as such. It’s time to Exodus.
